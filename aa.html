Our Story

Founded in 2008, AAA Power Enterprises began as a small family-run electrical service provider with a simple mission: to deliver reliable, affordable power solutions with uncompromising quality.

What started as a two-person operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted full-service electrical and maintenance companies, with a team of over 50 certified professionals serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Our growth has been fueled by our commitment to old-fashioned values: honesty, craftsmanship, and putting customers first. We don't just fix problems - we build lasting relationships.
